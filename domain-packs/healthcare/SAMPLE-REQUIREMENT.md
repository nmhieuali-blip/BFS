# Sample Requirement - Healthcare

## Requirement ID
REQ-HLTH-001

## Feature
Emergency Department Triage Prioritization

## Problem
Triage priority is recorded inconsistently, causing delays for critical patients.

## Goal
Ensure high-risk patients are prioritized and triage decisions are traceable.

## Constraints
- RBAC enforced
- Audit logging mandatory
- Triage updates must be visible in less than 2 seconds

# Sample Requirement - Finance

## Requirement ID
REQ-FIN-001

## Feature
Payment Reversal Workflow

## Problem
Reversals are processed inconsistently, creating ledger mismatch and audit risk.

## Goal
Provide controlled reversal flow with approval, rollback safety, and reconciliation outputs.

## Constraints
- Segregation of duties required
- Full audit trail required
- Reconciliation report generated daily

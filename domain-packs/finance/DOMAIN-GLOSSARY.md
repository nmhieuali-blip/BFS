# Finance Domain Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| General Ledger (GL) | Central accounting record |
| Journal Entry | Accounting record of a transaction |
| Reconciliation | Matching records across systems |
| Settlement | Final transfer of funds |
| Chargeback | Reversal initiated by payer/bank |
| Cut-off Time | Deadline for processing in period |
| Fiscal Period | Accounting reporting window |
| Segregation of Duties | Role separation for control |
